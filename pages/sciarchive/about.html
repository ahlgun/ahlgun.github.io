What is Science Archive?

Science Article is an internet archive for scientific
news and articles. It was created for easier access to
'the interesting side' of science. To be honest, most
scientific reports and studies are hard to follow
(and to some extent impossible). So this is my approach
to the problem, bringing you easy-to-follow content in a
simple manner. Content is added weekly and we try to keep
the page up to date as much as possible.
